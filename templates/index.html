Digital Exercise and Rehabilitation for Elderly Individuals.

VitalArcade is not just a game; it also serves as a means to maintain the psychomotor activity of older adults and introduce them to new technologies.
01

Mole smasher

Playing the Mole Smasher minigame can significantly enhance hand-eye coordination and reaction times, as players must quickly identify and target moles popping up in random locations. This engaging and fast-paced game also helps improve visual scanning and precision, making it a fun way to sharpen cognitive and motor skills.

VitalArcadeVR at Nursing Homes

Unlock a World of Fun and Fitness and Elevate the quality of life for seniors in residential communities. Designed specifically for older adults, VitalArcade offers a dynamic and engaging way to enhance mental acuity, sharpen attention, and improve physical conditioning.

Seniors can embark on exciting adventures, tackle brain-teasing puzzles, and participate in immersive games designed to stimulate the mind and body. From memory challenges that exercise cognitive functions to interactive experiences that promote hand-eye coordination and balance.
